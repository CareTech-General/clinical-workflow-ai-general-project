# 📁 docs/

This folder is where you plan and document your project in plain writing —
no coding needed! Start here before anything else.



## 📄 What's Inside

| `project-doc-template.md` | Your project blueprint — fill this out first before anything else |


## 🚀 Getting Started

1. Open `project-doc-template.md`
2. Rename a copy of it to `YOURNAME-project-doc.md`
3. Fill out every section — this is your project plan for the whole quarter
4. Come back and update it each week as your project grows



## 📝 What the Project Doc Covers

- **Clinical Problem** — What real problem are you solving?
- **Current Workflow** — How is it handled today without AI?
- **Proposed AI Solution** — What is your idea?
- **Workflow Integration** — Where does your AI fit in?
- **AI Inputs & Outputs** — What goes in and what comes out?
- **Intended Impact** — What improves if your solution works?
- **Weekly Progress Log** — Track what you do each week
